MedIQ is an intelligent healthcare monitoring application designed to improve hospital efficiency and patient care by enabling real-time monitoring of patient conditions. The system automatically detects critical patient status and instantly notifies doctors and nurses through a mobile-based alert system, enabling faster medical response and better decision-making.

The goal of MedIQ is to reduce response time during medical emergencies, automate patient monitoring, and enhance healthcare workflow through smart technology.
